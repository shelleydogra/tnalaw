ABOUT TANG & ASSOCIATES LAW OFFICE, LLC

Tang & Associates Law Office is a debt relief law office practicing in Chapter 7 Bankruptcy and Chapter 13 Bankruptcy to help you stop your debts, eliminate past due bills, stop harassing debt collection calls, stop foreclosures, stop foreclosure sale dates, stop repossessions, lift holds on frozen check accounts and savings accounts, remove boots from cars due to parking tickets, reinstate driver’s licenses. We can help you start a debt free life by filing a Chapter 7 Bankruptcy for you to wipe away all of your debts. We can help you keep your house and car by getting you caught up with all of your bills with a Chapter 13 Bankruptcy. If you are not looking for debt relief, and are in the market to purchase or sell a house, condo or any other real estate. I can assist you in purchasing or selling any and all real estate. Whether you need help in stopping your debts, anything from minor bills to major bills, or you need help with buying or selling real estate, Tang & Associates Law Office is here to help you.
