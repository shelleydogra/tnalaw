Tang and Associates

bankruptcy

I have 7 years of experience in Bankruptcy Law and I have worked on a wide variety of matters when dealing with Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 filings. I have helped clients ranging from liquidating some credit card debts, all the way to reorganizing debts to help clients stop a foreclosure sale of their home. I have always consulted with my clients and let them know what is best for them, even if it means to not file Bankruptcy. I understand that debt creates many stresses and I strive my best to assist my clients with their wishes.

real estate

I have 8 years of experience in Real Estate Law and I have worked on deals ranging from residential deals to commercial deals. I understand that buying a home is a major step in everyone's lives and it can create a lot of anxiety. In additional to buying a home, selling a home requires a knowledgeable attorney to ensure a successful transaction. I am here to help my clients navigate the home purchase, or home sale process, for a smooth transaction.
